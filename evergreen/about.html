About Us

Our Mission Statement

To advocate for and educate others on the importance of enviromentalism along with creating a school and country that is sustainably motivated and innovative.

Our Goals

Members FCHS Community

  1. Educate members about local ecology and environment.
  2. Promote and supply opportunities for sustainable living and zero waste alternatives.
  3. Supply members with adequate resources for future advocacy and activism regarding environmentalism.
  4. Provide access to community connections for future projects and initiatives.
  1. Educate the student body about environmental issues, and what they can do to help.
  2. Introduce energy-efficient and waste reduction protocols and equipment to the school.
  3. Receive recognition from the Virginia Department of Education as a Green Ribbon School.
  4. Create an informed and vocal student body in the environmental affairs of the school and surrounding community.
  1. Foster interest in the environment and base-level knowledge about conservation in elementary school students.
  2. Support and connect local ecological initiatives with the school and committee members.
  3. Protect local habitats and species from endangerment.
  4. Vocalize the importance of the environmentalism movement in Franklin County to local government.
